## 1. Executive Summary
Two controlled experiments were conducted to compare "Complex" (Full Detailer) vs. "Simplified" (Face Only) workflows.

* **Top Finding:** Disabling the Hand Detailer (MeshGraphormer/ControlNet) **increased the overall success rate by +5%** (from 23% to 28%).
* **Best Sampler:** **Euler Ancestral (Euler A)** is statistically superior, achieving a **~33% success rate** compared to **~18%** for standard Euler.
* **Best Scheduler:** Once the invasive Hand Detailer was removed, the performance gap between `sgm_uniform` and `simple` vanished. Both are now viable.

## 5. Final Recommendations ("The Golden Settings")

Based on this data, the Master Workflow should be locked to the following specification:

### 1. Main Generation
* **Sampler:** **`euler_ancestral`** (Required for 33% hit rate).
* **Scheduler:** **`simple`** (Preferred for softer contrast) OR `sgm_uniform` (Viable).
* **Strategy:** Do not mix `Euler` (standard) into production runs; it lowers the yield.

### 2. Refinement Strategy
* **Hand Refinement:** **OFF** in Master Workflow.
    * *Reason:* Euler A generates good hands natively. "Fixing" them causes more harm than good (artifacts).
    * *Protocol:* Only use "Fix Hands Workflow" (MeshGraphormer) manually for specific failed seeds.
* **Face Refinement:** **ON**.
    * **Settings:** Sampler `Euler` (Clean), CFG `4.0` (Safe), Steps `16-20`, Crop Factor `2.0`.
    * *Reason:* High sharpness, no color burn.
